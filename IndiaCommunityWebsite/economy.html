The Economy of India

RUPEE

The Major Businesses

As of right now, India is a major center for technology, manufacturing, and global business. Cities like Bengaluru became major technology hubs and host many global companies. Hyderabad and Chennai also lead in software, manufacturing, and research industries.

Crops and Tourism

Agriculture remains strong with crops such as tea, spices, coconut, and coffee. Tourism grows due to beaches, temples, natural landscapes in places like Kerala, and the well-known Taj Mahal.

The Currency of India

The Indian Rupee (₹) is the official currency of the entirety of India. It is roughly equivalent to $0.11 to $0.12. Because of the high-income from busy cities, most of the money is put towards central taxes.

Recent Economic Data/Developments

Global Economic Standing

India is currently the 5th largest economy in the world by nominal GDP.

Amount of Medicine Provided

India is the largest supplier of generic medicines globally.

Digital Public Infrastructure

India leads the world in processing real-time digital payments.

An Agricultural Powerhouse

India is the world's most largest supplier of milk and spices.
